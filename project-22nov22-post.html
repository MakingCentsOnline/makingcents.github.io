Guide on using BSC bridge and PancakeSwap to trade and earn $CENT

Making Cents
22 Nov 2022 • 6 min read

Introduction

What is a bridge? Bridges exist to connect blockchains, allowing the transfer of information and tokens between networks. The recent integration of $CENT on Binance Smart Chain (BSC) meant that $CENT can now be effectively transferred between the Vite and BSC blockchains. This will lead to better access to liquidity on BSC and also help bring in new users to $CENT and Vite ecosystem.

Content

This guide outlines the steps on how to use the BSC bridge and how to trade and earn $CENT on PancakeSwap. You may click on the link to skip directly to the section that interests you if you already have some knowledge of the other content.

  • Installing a BSC compatible wallet and importing $CENT token
  • Performing cross-chain transfer of $CENT from Vite to BSC
  • Trading $CENT on PancakeSwap
  • Earning $CENT on PancakeSwap Liquidity Pools
  • Performing cross-chain transfer of $CENT from BSC to Vite
  • Staking your LP token on Vite chain to amplify returns

.  .  .

Installing a BSC compatible wallet and importing $CENT token

1. Please install any BSC compatible wallet from the recommended list HERE. We recommend using the MetaMask wallet. Download and install using the following links:

  • For Android users: Click HERE
  • For Apple users: Click HERE

2. Connect MetaMask to BSC network. Go to “Settings” in your MetaMask wallet to add new network. Fill up the following details in the fields and click on “Add”.

  • Network Name: Smart Chain
  • New RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
  • ChainID: 56
  • Symbol: BNB
  • Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com

Step 2 (as above)

3. Import the $CENT token. The $CENT contract is: 0xee37A338e781b9b86f3E79fBF09D6032D2DBc109. You can verify the contract HERE

Step 3 (as above)

.  .  .

Performing cross-chain transfer of $CENT from Vite to BSC

1. You should already have your Vite wallet installed. See guide here on how to set up Vite wallet: https://medium.com/@vivafi/getting-started-with-viva-beginners-guide-to-set-up-vite-wallet-and-trade-on-vitex-exchange-17770ed69e1c

2. Open your Vite wallet and select CENT-000. Select “Cross-Chain Withdraw” and input the BSC (BEP-20) address that you wish to transfer $CENT to. Please note that the minimum withdrawal amount is 3,000,000,000 $CENT and that there will be a fee of 2,000,000,000 $CENT associated for the withdrawal. This is wholly paid towards XGate for maintaining the cross-chain gateway, Making Cents does not receive any part of this fee. This fee may be adjusted in the future based on the price of $CENT.

Step 2 (as above)

3. Select “Withdraw”. Within a few minutes, your $CENT (BSC) will be transferred to your BSC compatible wallet as $CENT (BEP-20).

4. You are now ready to trade on PancakeSwap.

.  .  .

Trading $CENT on PancakeSwap

1. Visit PancakeSwap using your MetaMask browswer at: https://pancakeswap.finance/.

2. Click “Connect Wallet”.

Steps 1 and 2 (as above)

3. Select MetaMask and approve the connection to PancakeSwap. You may also follow similar instructions if you are using other wallets.

Step 3 (as above)

4. On the main page, select the “Trade” tab. Select the token to swap.

5. Click on “Manage tokens”.

6. Toggle to the “Tokens” tab. Fill in the $CENT contract address (0xee37A338e781b9b86f3E79fBF09D6032D2DBc109) and click “Import”.

7. Check “I understand”, then click “Import”.

Steps 4 to 7 (as above)

8. Once completed, you are ready to trade $CENT against another currency.

9. You can access the trading pair via this link as well. https://pancakeswap.finance/swap?outputCurrency=0xee37A338e781b9b86f3E79fBF09D6032D2DBc109

.  .  .

Earning $CENT on PancakeSwap Liquidity Pools

1. Visit PancakeSwap using your MetaMask browswer at: https://pancakeswap.finance/.

2. Select the “Trade” tab and click on “Liquidity”.

3. Click on “Add Liquidity”. If you have already imported the $CENT from previous section, you may skip following steps 4 to 6. Otherwise, please follow through.

4. Click on “Manage tokens”.

5. Toggle to the “Tokens” tab. Fill in the $CENT contract address (0xee37A338e781b9b86f3E79fBF09D6032D2DBc109) and click “Import”.

6. Check “I understand”, then click “Import”.

Steps 4 to 6(as above)

7. Select the currency pair that you wish to add liquidity for. The first liquidity pool for $CENT on Pancakeswap is $CENT / $BUSD.

8. Enter the amount of $CENT or $BUSD you wish to add to the pool. PancakeSwap will match the value of both currencies automatically. You will also be able to see your staked tokens as a proportion of the entire pool. As a liquidity provider, you will earn on trading fees of the trading pair. Your earnings are influenced by:

  • The proportion of your contributions to the liquidity pool i.e. if your staked tokens is 10% of the entire pool, you will earn 10% of all trading fees collected.
  • Activity of the trading pair i.e. the more people who trades, the more fees collected, the more you earn.

9. Click “Enable CENT” and “Enable BUSD”, and then “Supply” to proceed.

10. After you have completed staking your tokens, you will receive LP (Liquidity Pool) tokens in return. The LP tokens become your claim to your share of the pool’s assets, should you wish to un-stake your tokens at a later time.

Steps 7 to 10 (as above)

.  .  .

Performing cross-chain transfer of $CENT from BSC to Vite

1. Open your Vite wallet and select CENT-000.

2. Select “Cross-Chain Deposit”.

3. Copy your cross-chain deposit address.

4. Enter your cross-chain deposit address on MetaMask, and transfer the amount of $CENT you want. Please note that there is a minimum deposit of 1,000,000,000 $CENT. Any amount lower than minimum deposit transferred will not be recoverable.

.  .  .

Staking your LP token on Vite chain to amplify returns

We will also be partnering with Viva Staking Pools where you could stake your CENTBUSD LP token to earn even more $CENT. You can do so by following the instructions below.

1. Add the CENTBUSD LP token on your MetaMask. Click ‘Import tokens’

2. Add the following contract address and import the token: 0x292B2118812c9a43d8994271a81DbCe870EeAf6B

3. You should be able to see the CENTBUSD LP token and balance in your wallet. To withdraw your CENTBUSD LP token onto the Vite chain, open your Vite wallet, add the CENTBUSD-000 token to your wallet.

4. Select the CENTBNB-000 token.

5. Select “Cross-Chain Deposit”.

6. Copy your cross-chain deposit address.

7. Enter your cross-chain deposit address on MetaMask, and transfer the amount of LP token you want.

8. After the LP token has been received in your Vite wallet, head over to app.vivafi.org, connect your wallet and stake the LP token to start earning rewards in $CENT!

Step 8 (as above)
